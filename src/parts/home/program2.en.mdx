### ONLINE CONNECT GROUPS

Connect with American English speakers online! Our Connect program will help you grow in conversational fluency and listening comprehension. Meet people all over the world and grow your English skills! Connect Groups includes one 30 minute interview with native English speakers each week and one 90 minute coaching session with one of our trained coaches. This program is for students who are at an intermediate or advanced level.
